# Currency Systems

Gold, Justice Points, Valor Points, and Honor Points (future).

## Gold

- **Primary currency** earned from quests, monster kills, vendor sales
- Scales from copper/silver (early game) to gold (endgame)
- Uses:
  - Ability training
  - Gear repairs
  - Consumables
  - Profession recipes
  - Mounts
  - Talent respec costs

## Justice Points

- **Cap:** 4,000 points
- **Purpose:** Alternative to RNG loot drops (bad luck protection)
- **Sources:**
  - Daily quests
  - Heroic dungeon completion
  - Raid participation
- **Uses:**
  - Purchasing Rare and Epic gear from vendors

## Valor Points

- **Weekly cap:** 1,000 points (encourages consistent play)
- **Purpose:** Best-in-slot gear acquisition (engagement driver)
- **Sources:**
  - Weekly quests
  - Raid bosses
  - High-tier content
- **Uses:**
  - Purchasing best-in-slot Epic gear

## Honor Points (Future PvP System)

- Earned from PvP activities
- Used for PvP-specific gear with resilience stat
- Not implemented in initial release
