# Achievement System

Achievement categories and rewards.

## Achievement Categories

1. **Leveling** -- Reach specific levels, complete zone quests
2. **Dungeon** -- Complete all dungeons, speed clears, no-death runs
3. **Raid** -- Boss kills, tier completions, hard mode clears
4. **Professions** -- Reach max skill, craft specific items
5. **Collections** -- Mounts, pets, transmog appearances
6. **Exploration** -- Discover all zones, find hidden areas
7. **Combat** -- Kill milestones, critical hit records
8. **Wealth** -- Accumulate gold, spend thresholds

## Achievement Rewards

- **Achievement Points** -- For leaderboard ranking
- **Titles** -- Displayed with character name
- **Unique mounts and pets**
- **Cosmetic rewards** -- Transmog gear, effects
- **Paragon bonus points** -- For major achievement milestones
